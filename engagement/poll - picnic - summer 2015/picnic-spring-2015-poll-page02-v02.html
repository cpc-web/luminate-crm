Where should you have a perfect NYC picnic?

CHERRY HILL

CHERRY HILL

Lay out a blanket for your homemade feast on the lush, grassy slopes of Cherry Hill. With views overlooking the Lake and the Ramble, this secluded spot is perfect for a romantic picnic for two!

SHEEP MEADOW

SHEEP MEADOW

Classy choice! Consider hosting your next picnic at Sheep Meadow, the Park’s largest lawn without ballfields. As one of the Park’s designated Quiet Zones, it allows you to sit in peace while you enjoy one of the Park’s best views of the Manhattan skyline.

SHARE ON TWITTER

GREAT LAWN

GREAT LAWN

You surely won’t be alone in ordering delivery on the Great Lawn. On this grassy expanse, New Yorkers of all stripes can picnic in style while watching a game on one of the Lawn’s baseball diamonds. When your food arrives, snap the perfect Instagram shot!

SHARE ON FACEBOOK

SHARE ON TWITTER

CEDAR HILL

CEDAR HILL

We have just the spot for you! Your croissants and cakes will taste especially sweet under one of the red cedars on the crest of Cedar Hill. On this sunny, hillside meadow surrounded by trees, your picnic will feel worlds away from the everyday.
